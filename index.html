My Favorite Movies

The Amazing Spider-Man 2

This movie has been my favorite since I was a kid because Spider-Man has always been my favorite superhero. I watch it whenever I feel sad or bored, and it never fails to lift my mood. Even now, The Amazing Spider-Man 2 is still my go-to movie, and it continues to heal my inner child. What I really love about it is how Spider-Man shows strength, responsibility, and resilience despite everything he goes through.

The Maze Runner

My friend recommended The Maze Runner, and at first, it didn’t really pique my interest since I’m more into animated movies. But when I finally watched it, I became really interested in the plot. The story was intense and full of mystery, which kept me hooked the whole time. I was also surprised to find out that it had a second and third movie, so I ended up watching the whole series.

Harry Potter and The Goblet of Fire

Harry Potter has always been on my watch list every Christmas. I really enjoy watching it on Christmas Eve because it feels very nostalgic. I don’t just like this movie, I love it. My best friend and I always get on a call on Discord just to watch it together, which makes the experience even more special.

The Love of My Life

Ysabel Adap | Baba <3

Ysabel is very special to me. She changed the way I see the world and everything around me. She was there for me during one of my lowest points, and she has never left my side since the day we met.

We first got to know each other when we were on the same volleyball team, where I was her captain. At first, I didn’t really have any interest in her, and our relationship was purely as captain and teammate. But as we spent more time together during practices, our bond gradually grew. Along the way, we developed feelings for each other, and now we’ve come this far.

I love her so much, and she is one of the most precious people in my life.

My Hobbies

Playing Pickleball

Playing pickleball became one of my favorite things to do, and it eventually became my favorite sport. I started playing when my brother invited me to join him. Of course, it was very hard at first, but as time went by, I kept practicing, and my skill level in open plays improved from novice to advanced.

Working-Out

I started working out when I was in Grade 8, and having a good physique became one of my dreams. I have been consistent in training my body, and until now, I am still working out to achieve the physique I want.

Biking

Biking is one of the hobbies that I really love; it has brought joy and freedom into my life. Whenever I’m on the road, pedaling and sprinting—I feel free. It has been my go-to whenever I’m stressed or overwhelmed. It can be dangerous, but it’s what keeps me going. I also bike to school every day as part of my workout routine, and I often go out to different places with my friends and classmates. That’s how much I love biking.
